Soundtrack for Nguyễn Ngọc Thảo Ly's «Memory Lane»

Scottish Documentary Institute

Created Oct 1, 2019 - Last updated: Oct 1, 2019

soundtrack

If you have been in the spotlight all your life, it is hard to give it up. Lucky, Mr Le Quang Mui, her 90 year old husband and life-long singing partner is always ready to indulge her. But their memory plays tricks and leads to some serious disagreements. ‘Memory Lane’ is a quirky portrait of elderly love in Vietnam peppered with a bit of Tuong, traditional folk singing. Lý Trang’s soundtrack for the movie once again unveils the traditional elements in Tuong.
